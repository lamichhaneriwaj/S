About Us

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Since 1985, Greenwood Academy has been at the forefront of educational excellence, nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future.

Message from Principal

"At Greenwood Academy, we believe every child is unique and capable of achieving greatness. Our commitment is to provide an environment where students can explore, learn, and grow into confident, compassionate leaders of tomorrow. We foster not just academic excellence, but also character development, critical thinking, and global citizenship. Together, we are building a community of lifelong learners who will make a positive impact on the world."
Dr. Sarah Mitchell Principal, Greenwood Academy

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that empowers students to become confident, creative, and compassionate global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that inspires excellence and innovation in every student we serve.
